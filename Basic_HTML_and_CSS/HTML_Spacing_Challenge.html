Psalm 23

The Lord is my sheeperd,
I shall not want
He makes me to lie down
In greeen pastures
He leads me beside the still waters
He restores my soul
He leads me in path of righteousness
for His name sake
Even though I walk through the valley shadow of death
 I shall fear no evil
for though hath with me
thy rod and thy staff they comfort me
thy laid a table before me
in the presence of my enemies
Thou annoited my head with oil
my cup runs over
Surely God's goodness and mercies
shall follow me
All the days of my life
And I shall dwell in the house
of the Lord
forever and ever
Amen
